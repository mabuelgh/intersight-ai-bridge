LuminaTech Solutions

LuminaTech Solutions is a pioneering technology firm specializing in advanced holographic projection and interactive AI interfaces. Founded in 2028, the company has quickly established itself as a leader in immersive digital experiences for both enterprise and consumer markets. Their flagship product, the "HoloConnect," allows for seamless 3D holographic communication and collaborative workspace environments, revolutionizing remote work and entertainment. LuminaTech is committed to pushing the boundaries of spatial computing and making virtual interactions indistinguishable from reality.
