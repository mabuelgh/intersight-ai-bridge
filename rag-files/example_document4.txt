Chronos Innovations

Chronos Innovations is a research and development firm focused on next-generation energy storage and temporal data analytics. Since its inception in 2025, Chronos has been at the forefront of developing compact, high-density quantum batteries for electric vehicles and grid-scale energy solutions. Additionally, their "TemporalFlow" AI platform offers predictive modeling and anomaly detection by analyzing vast datasets across time, providing critical insights for industries ranging from finance to climate science. Chronos Innovations aims to unlock new possibilities in energy efficiency and foresight.
